Jorge Salgado Miranda, individual professional based in Mexico City. Contact: jorgesalgadomiranda@protonmail.com. Registered corporate vehicle for billed engagements is Capdesis; for Capdesis-specific processing see that company's own notice.

2. Data I collect

  • Contact form submissions: name, email, company (optional), topic of interest and message body. Submitted through Web3Forms to my inbox.
  • Booking data if you schedule a call via Cal.com: name, email, time slot, optional notes. Processed by Cal.com under their own terms.
  • Analytics: aggregate, privacy-preserving page-view data via Umami (self-hosted, no cookies, no personal identifiers) and Cloudflare Web Analytics (zero-cookie, edge-native). Neither issues cookies, so no consent banner is needed.
  • Edge and security logs via Cloudflare for basic DDoS and bot protection. IP addresses may be briefly retained for abuse mitigation; they are not used for marketing or profiling.

3. Why I use it

  • To reply to consulting inquiries (lawful basis: legitimate interest, or your explicit request).
  • To schedule and prepare calls (basis: contract performance).
  • To understand which content is read and improve the site (basis: legitimate interest; only aggregate data).

4. Retention

Contact form emails stay in my ProtonMail inbox indefinitely unless you request deletion, so that I can reopen a thread if you reach out again years later. Analytics data is retained by Umami and Cloudflare per their default policies; none of it identifies individuals.

5. Sharing

I do not sell data. I share it only with the tools listed above, acting as processors. No marketing mailing list, no ad networks, no data brokers.

6. Your rights

Mexican (LFPDPPP) and EU (GDPR) frameworks both apply because my site accepts international traffic. You can request access, correction, portability, deletion, or object to processing by emailing me. I reply within 7 calendar days and always within 30.

7. Security

TLS 1.2+, HSTS preload, CSP, Cloudflare bot fight mode, rotating FTP credentials on the host, and no server-side application on this domain (all content is static HTML served from Hostinger).

8. Cookies

This site does not set any first-party cookies. Third-party analytics are cookieless. Cal.com may set cookies on its own domain when you book a call; that is subject to Cal.com's policy.

9. Changes to this notice

If material changes occur, the "Last updated" line above reflects them. Minor wording changes are not separately notified.
